MEDICAL PRESCRIPTION GENERATOR

This application generates the prescription after detecting the spoken symptom.
It can be used by a doctor in the clinic to generate prescription for common symptoms.
The doctor doesn't have to write it repeatedly, and they can speak the symptom and the prescription will be generated automatically.This saves time and paper which can be used to attend more patients.

GUIDELINES

The main page consists of three buttons:

  • ABOUT
    •      - it will lead you to the page where the guidelines are mentioned.

  • TEST
    •      - it will lead you to another page where you can speak the symptom by clicking on the button.

  • TRAIN
    •      - it will train for new speaker, you'll have to speak 20 utterances of each word and a new set of lambdas will be created in "new-lambda folder" which you can copy to main folder and test the model for your voice, it takes a little time to speak and train the model.
